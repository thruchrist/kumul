User Guide

Finding a job in Papua New Guinea is now as easy as sending a text message. Follow the steps below to get started with KUMUL AI.

1. Save the Contact

First, save our official WhatsApp number to your phone contacts.

+675 XXXXXXXX

We recommend naming the contact "KUMUL AI" so you can find it easily later.

2. Say Hello

Open WhatsApp and send a message to the number you just saved.

Hi

The bot will wake up and greet you. You can also type Hello or Help to see options.

3. Tell Us About Yourself

KUMUL is smart! It needs to know your skills to find you the right job. Just chat naturally.

Example Conversations

"I am a Driver in Lae."
"I am an Accountant looking for work in Port Moresby."

KUMUL will automatically remember your Profession and Location for future searches.

4. Search for Jobs

Once KUMUL knows who you are, just ask for jobs.

"Find me a job"

The AI will search the web and return the most relevant, recent listings.

Location Sharing

You can also share your Location Pin via WhatsApp. This helps the AI find jobs specifically near your geographic area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free?

Yes. KUMUL AI is completely free to use for job seekers in Papua New Guinea.

Does KUMUL save my data?

Yes. We securely save your phone number, profession, and location history to provide the best matches. We never sell your data to third parties.

What if the bot doesn't reply?

KUMUL AI might be sleeping if there is high traffic. Please wait a few minutes and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.

Can I use Tok Pisin?

Yes! KUMUL understands English and Tok Pisin. You can chat in the language you are most comfortable with.
