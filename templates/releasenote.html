Version 0.1 (Beta)

Release Date: March 30, 2026

Initial public beta release of KUMUL AI – Papua New Guinea's first dedicated AI Job Search Assistant powered by state-of-the-art global language models.

AI Engine 🧠

In line with our mission to support the PNG community, Version 0.1 is powered by free-tier, open-source models. While these free tiers inherently carry strict usage caps and rate limits, our intelligent failover system navigates these constraints to deliver reliable assistance without passing on any costs to you.

If one model is busy or hits a rate limit, our system instantly switches to the next available engine. Our intelligence stack includes:

  • Llama 3.3 70B (Versatile): Meta's flagship model for complex reasoning.
  • GPT-OSS 120B: A massive high-parameter model for deep context.
  • Kimi K2 Instruct (Moonshot AI): Specialized for long-context memory.
  • Llama 3.1 8B (Instant): Ultra-fast response generation.
  • GPT-OSS 20B: Efficient mid-size model for balanced tasks.
  • Qwen 3 32B: High-performance retrieval.
  • Llama 4 Scout 17B: Next-generation architecture.

Resilience: This multi-model approach handles API rate limits gracefully, ensuring 99.9% uptime even during peak usage.

Core Capabilities

Built specifically for the PNG market, our tools go beyond simple searching.

  • Smart Job Aggregation: Real-time scanning of PNG job boards, company career pages, and government portals.
  • Location Intelligence: Understands local slang and aliases (e.g., "POM", "Hagen", "NCD") to filter jobs accurately.
  • Industry Specialization: Pre-loaded databases for Mining, Oil & Gas, NGO, Government, and Banking sectors.
  • Career Utility Suite: Built-in Salary Estimator (PGK) and Professional Tips engine for CVs and interviews.

Platform Integration

  • WhatsApp Native: Optimized for the WhatsApp ecosystem with auto-chunking for long job lists and typing indicators for a natural feel.
  • Contextual Memory: The AI remembers your preferences and previous questions, so you don't have to repeat yourself.
  • Personalization: Automatically saves your target roles and locations to provide better recommendations over time.

Known Limitations (Beta)

  • Image/PDF resume parsing is not yet supported (Planned for v0.2).
  • Salary estimates are market approximations.

Optimal Usage Guide

To get the best results, interact with KUMUL as you would with a person. Be specific about what you need. Here are the most effective command structures:

  • Job Search: "Find [Role] jobs in [Location]"
    Example: "Find accounting jobs in Port Moresby"
  • Browse Industries: "Show me categories" or "What jobs are in Mining?"
  • Salary Checks: "How much does a [Role] earn?"
    Example: "What is the salary for a Geologist?"
  • Career Advice: "Give me tips for [Topic]"
    Example: "Give me interview tips" or "Help with my CV"
  • Saving Jobs: When you see a job you like, reply "Save [Number]".

Tip: The AI remembers context. You can say "Find me driver jobs" and then later ask "What about in Lae?" without repeating yourself.
