Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critical to us. This policy outlines how KUMUL AI handles user data.

Information We Collect

We collect information provided directly via WhatsApp, including:

  • Phone Number
  • Job Preferences (Profession, Skills)
  • Location (Text or Coordinates)
  • Message History

How We Use Information

Data is used solely to:

  • Match users with relevant job opportunities.
  • Improve the AI's understanding of the PNG job market.
  • Maintain conversation context.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in a private database. Access to the database is strictly limited to the core development team.
