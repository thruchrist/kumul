About KUMUL AI

KUMUL AI is a community-driven initiative designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employment opportunities in Papua New Guinea using advanced Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

To democratize access to job market information. We believe every Papua New Guinean deserves access to real-time job opportunities without needing high-end devices or expensive data plans.

Built entirely on WhatsApp, KUMUL AI meets users where they already are, removing friction from the job search process.

Key Features

Intelligent Job Matching

Powered by Large Language Models (Llama 3.3), KUMUL understands context. It doesn't just match keywords; it understands professions, skills, and geographic locations.

WhatsApp Native

No apps to download. No complex websites. KUMUL runs entirely within WhatsApp, making it accessible to anyone with a smartphone.

Privacy First

User data is securely stored and never sold. We use data solely to improve job matching accuracy.

Technology Stack

KUMUL AI is built on a robust, modern open-source stack:

  • Core AI: Llama 3.3 70B (via Groq)
  • Backend: Python, FastAPI, LangChain
  • Database: PostgreSQL (Neon)
  • Hosting: Render.com
  • Search: Tavily Search API
